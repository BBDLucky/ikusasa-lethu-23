What is gender base violence ?

Gender-Based violence can include sexual,physical,economic harm inflicted in public or in private and public .It inludes threats of violence coercion and manipulation. People who are most in risk are woman and girls.Those in crisis settings are at a double disadvantage due to their gender and situations .Woman and girls from other diverse and marginalised communities face an even greater risk where gender inequality combines with other forms of oppression

Causes of gender based violence

  • socio economic issues
  • cultural dynamics
  • poverty
